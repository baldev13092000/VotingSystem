Why your vote matters ?

If you ever think that just one vote in a sea of millions cannot make much of a difference, It makes a lot.
Because of this, and the fact that voter quite literally have the future of the country in their hands, We urge any and all people reading this to realize how big a part of democracy voting is, and to use the opportunities given to them to make a difference in India.

About us

Smart voting system will be secure and reduce the inconvenience of voters.Using Blockchain technology the system will be made more secure and free from any frauds.Basically the system will eradicate dummy votes by use of smart cards. The smart card will have the biometric information(retinal images) and when swiped ,the local machine will check for the correct biometrics of the person.
